At What Age Should I Begin Giving My Child an Allowance?

Sep 16th, 2010 | Comments

Never.  You shouldn’t ever just give your kids an allowance.  What message are you teaching them?  That it’s ok to mooch?  My 3 yr old (yes 3) gets one dollar when he helps to pick up his toys.  Why $1?  Because he can’t swallow it like he can swallow change.  Then we put it in his piggy bank and when he gets a few bucks I’ll take some out and re-use them. 
via mint.com

Seeing this answer made me laugh. We give our kids a buck a week to put in their piggy bank… and we never “take some out and re-use them.”

Marine Mammal Center

Jul 12th, 2010 | Comments

My boys and I went to the Marine Mammal Center in the Marin Headlands
(http://www.marinemammalcenter.org) this weekend. Got to see some
elephant seals and California sea lions. The most surprising thing
was the postmortem lab where you can see vets disecting marine mammals
through a window. My 5 y/o boys were glued to the window as the vet
hacked through a sea lions’ skull to expose the brain, skinned the
skull, and took the eye balls out of its socket. Most of the adults
who watched just about gagged, but not my guys.
